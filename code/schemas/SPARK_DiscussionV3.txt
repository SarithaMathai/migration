    sparkDiscussions: SPARK_BulkDiscussionInputV3!
    attachments: [SPARK_BulkAttachmentInput]
  ): SPARK_BulkDiscussionOutputV3
  updateDiscussionV3(
    id: ID!

  ): SPARK_Discussion

  ): SPARK_Discussion
